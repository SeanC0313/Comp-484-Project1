Enhypen consists of 7 members each with their own unique personalities and talents. The members are Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, Jungwon, and Ni-ki. Each member brings something special to the group, contributing to their overall success and popularity in the K-pop industry.

Jungwon

  • Stage Name: Jungwon
  • Birth Name: Yang Jungwon
  • Birth Date: February 9, 2004
  • Positions: Leader, Lead Dancer, Vocalist
  • Nationality: South Korean
  • He was born in Yeongdeungpo-gu, Seoul, South Korea. He grew up in Gwanak-gu.
  • Jungwon has an older sister born in 2002.
  • Education: Namgang High School, Hanlim Multi Art School.
  • Training Period: 1 year, 4 months.
  • His microphone color is gray.
  • He ranked 1st in the final episode of I-land with 1,417,620 votes.

Heeseung

  • Stage Name: Heeseung
  • Birth Name: Lee Heeseung
  • Birth Date: October 15, 2001
  • Positions: Main Vocalist
  • Nationality: South Korean
  • He was born in Uiwang, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea.
  • Heeseung has an older brother born in 1999.
  • Education: Gwangnam High School.
  • Training Period: 3 years, 1 month.
  • His microphone color is red.
  • He ranked 5th in the final episode of I-land with 1,137,323 votes.

Jay

  • Stage Name: Jay
  • Birth Name: Park Jongseong
  • Birth Date: April 20th, 2002
  • Positions: Lead Vocalist
  • Nationality: Korean-American
  • His hometown is Seattle, Washington, U.S but he moved to Korea when he was 9 years old.
  • Jay is an only child.
  • Education: Hanlim Arts High School (Practical Dance Department).
  • Training Period: 2 years, 11 months.
  • His microphone color is green.
  • He ranked 2nd in the final episode of I-land with 1,182,889 votes.

Jake

  • Stage Name: Jake
  • Birth Name: Sim Jaeyun
  • Birth Date: November 15th, 2002
  • Positions: Lead Vocalist
  • Nationality: Korean-Australian
  • He was born in Seoul, South Korea but raised in Australia since he was nine years old.
  • Jake has an older brother (born in 2000).
  • Education: Edge Hill State School, St Peters Lutheran College, Dwight School Seoul.
  • Training Period: 9 months.
  • His microphone color is blue.
  • He ranked 3rd in the final episode of I-land with 1,179,633 votes.

Sunghoon

  • Stage Name: Sunghoon
  • Birth Name: Park Sunghoon
  • Birth Date: December 8th, 2002
  • Positions: Visual, Vocalist
  • Nationality: South Korean
  • He was born in Cheonan, Chungcheongnam-do, South Korea.
  • Sunghoon has a younger sister, named Park Yeji (born in 2007).
  • Education: Pangok High School.
  • Training Period: 2 years, 1 month.
  • His microphone color is white.
  • He ranked 6th in the final episode of I-land with 1,088,413 votes.

Sunoo

  • Stage Name: Sunoo
  • Birth Name: Kim Sunoo

  • Nationality: South Korean

  • Education: Chilbo Middle School, Chilbo High School, Hanlim Arts High School.
